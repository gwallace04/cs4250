\hwnumber{7}{23(a and b)}

\textbf{(a)}

\begin{align*}
	&2 * (b - 1) - 1 > 0 \\
	&2 * (b - 1) > 1 \\
	&(b - 1) > \frac{1}{2}\\
	&b > \frac{3}{2}
\end{align*}

So the weakest precondition is $b > \frac{3}{2}$
\medskip

\textbf{(b)}

\begin{align*}
	&\frac{c + 10}{3} > 6\\
	&c + 10 > 18 \\
	&c > 8
\end{align*}

So the weakest precondition is $c > 8$.
